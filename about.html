TALENTED, PASSIONATE, AND AMBITIOUS SAMANTHA DISCOVERED HER LOVE FOR ART MANY YEARS AGO WHILE GROWING UP IN MIAMI, FLORIDA.

IN 2017, SAMANTHA TRANSLATED HER LOVE FOR ART INTO HER LOVE FOR BEAUTY AS SHE BECAME A MAKEUP ARTIST. SAMANTHA WAS ADMITTED INTO THE GLAM SQUAD PROGRAM AFTER COLLEGE WHERE SHE BEGAN TO MASTER COLOR THEORY, SYMMETRY, TEXTURE, AND INNOVATION. HERE THE FEELING OF MAKING SOMEONE SEE THE BEAUTY WITHIN THEMSELVES BECAME HER GREATEST PASSION. HER STYLE FOCUSES ON THE FUSION OF FLAWLESS SKIN, MODERN BEAUTY ELEMENTS, AND ABSTRACT EXPRESSIONISM.

SAMANTHA BRINGS A CALMING SPIRIT AND VIBRANT ENERGY TO SET THAT MAKES FOR AN OPEN AND CREATIVE SPACE. HER FUN AND SWEET PERSONALITY WILL LEAVE YOU FEELING AS THOUGH YOU’VE KNOWN HER FOR MANY YEARS. TODAY HER AMBITION TO BE AN INSPIRATION WITHIN THE WORLD OF BEAUTY IS STRONGER THAN EVER.

SAMANTHA IS CURRENTLY BASED IN MIAMI WHERE SHE WORKS AS A MAKEUP ARTIST FOR COMMERCIAL FASHION EDITORIALS, AND PRIVATE CLIENTS. HAIR STYLING PROVIDED UPON REQUEST.
